News

August 29, 2015 - On August 28th, the Gator Club of Naples (“GCN”) held its first annual Golf Tournament to benefit the GCN Scholarship Fund. With over forty players in attendance, the Golf Tournament was an absolute success! All proceeds from the Golf Tournament will be used solely for the purpose of providing scholarships to Collier County area students attending UF. The Tournament would not have been possible without the support of its generous sponsors and donors: Vasari Country Club, Naples Acura, Audi Naples, VW of Naples, DeMarco Tile, Calusa Construction, Mike Horn Insurance, Hawthorn PNC Family Wealth, Studio A.D. Architect, Kathi Schweikhardt (Realtor) of Premiere Realty Plus Co., Bluewater of South Florida, Inc., Ferguson Waterworks, CFA Society, Woodruff Institute, the Naples Zoo at Caribbean Gardens, Royal Woods Golf and Country Club, Hunter's Ridge Country Club, Southwest Florida Brewery Tours, Broken Niblick, Outback Steakhouse, Tacos n' Tequila, The Golf Guys, Joey D's, Dick's Sporting Goods, PBS Construction, and Daane Properties. Please make sure to show your appreciation for these local establishments by considering each of them first for any goods or services you might require. Thank you all for your immense help and support! We look forward to making next year's Golf Tournament even better (which, considering how amazing this year's was, seems impossible)! GO GATORS!!!!

August 21, 2015 - The Gator Club of Naples is now offering t-shirts for sale. The shirts will have the logo set forth below and the price of each shirt is only $15.00. All net proceeds from the t-shirt sales will be directly deposited into the Gator Club of Naples Scholarship Fund. August 6, 2015 - On August 5, 2015, the Gator Club of Naples hosted its second annual Send-Off for those Collier County area students who will be entering their freshman year at the University of Florida beginning the Summer of 2015, Fall of 2015, and Spring of 2016 semesters. These “Baby Gators” are, by far, the most impressive and the most intelligent young adults that the Club has had the pleasure of meeting. Further, it was during this Send-Off Reception that the Gator Club of Naples held its first Scholarship Award Ceremony! Over thirty-two candidates applied for scholarship, but only three were selected after an intense and thorough review and selection process. The Gator Club of Naples would like to congratulate the following scholarship recipients: Johann Charelus, Krista Grajo, and Keri Mulhern! Congratulations! Also, the Gator Club of Naples would like to thank its generous Send-Off Reception sponsors: Quarles & Brady LLP and Moe’s Southwest Grill. Thank you and go Gators!

August 1, 2015 - The Gator Club of Naples has adopted its namesake, the (alli)gators, of the Alligator Bay at the Naples Zoo at Caribbean Gardens. With the assistance of the Gator Club’s contribution, the care for and support of the alligators will be provided for, for the year beginning August 2015. For more information on our gator friends, please visit the Naples Zoo’s website at: http://napleszoo.com/Visitor_Info/zoo-events/alligatorbay.html. “If you’re not a Gator, you’re Gator Bait!”
