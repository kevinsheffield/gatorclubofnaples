Membership

The best way to become involved is to become a member of the University of Florida Alumni Association (UFAA); you need not be an alum or student to apply. Anyone who is supportive of the academic, philanthropic, and athletic endeavors of the University of Florida is more than welcome to become a member. Once you become a member of the UFAA, you will automatically become a member of the Gator Club of Naples. Further, a portion of the membership dues paid to the UFAA will be remitted to the Gator Club of Naples to be directly allocated to its Scholarship Fund. To become a member of the UFAA, please register at http://www.ufalumni.ufl.edu/join/. If you have any questions about membership, or need additional information, please contact the Gator Club of Naples at membership@gatorclubofnaples.com.

Contact Us

For additional information regarding:

  • Events, please email events@gatorclubofnaples.com;
  • Scholarships, please email scholarships@gatorclubofnaples.com; and/or
  • Membership, please email membership@gatorclubofnaples.com.
For all other inquiries, please email the Gator Club of Naples at info@gatorclubofnaples.com.
