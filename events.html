Vasari Country Club

Scholarship Fundraising Event, First Annual Golf Tournament

The Gator Club of Naples is hosting its first Golf Tournament on August 28, 2015 at the Vasari Country Club. May 23, 2015 at 9:30 AM

1590 Goodlette-Frank Road

Naples, FL 34102

International Gator Day, Volunteering at the Naples Zoo at Caribbean Gardens

In May, 2015, Gator Clubs from around the world will unite to utilize the size and spirit of the Gator Nation to positively impact local communities.  Each Club will take on a project that is important to their community and will volunteer the time and talents of its members to serve others.  This year, a wide variety of non-profit organizations will receive an assist from the Gator Nation. The Gator Nation is never stronger than when we work together for a common cause.  Please join the Gator Club of Naples on May 23rd to show the world we are All Strong for our Community. The Gator Club of Naples will volunteer at The Naples Zoo.  Our group will be helping out with the general maintenance and operation of the daily zoo activities from 9:30 AM to 1:00 PM.  Gator volunteers will meet outside the main entrance to the Zoo.

Please wear your Orange and Blue and join the Gator Club of Naples in giving back to our community.

Persons interested in volunteering should e-mail Joel Corley, Gator Club of Naples VP of Communications and Membership, at jgc1029@comcast.net by May 15th.
