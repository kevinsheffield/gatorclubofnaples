GATOR CLUB OF NAPLES T-SHIRTS
Each t-shirt will have the logo, as pictured below, emblazoned on the front portion of the shirt. Each shirt is $15.00. To purchase a shirt, please email the Gator Club of Naples at info@gatorclubofnaples with the following information: name, email, phone number, number of shirts, size of each shirt, proposed method of payment (credit card, paypal, cash, or check), and whether you wish to pick up the shirt or have the same delivered. If you wish to have the shirt(s) mailed to you, you will be charged the actual cost of shipping; otherwise, you may pick up the shirt at an agreed upon board meeting. Further, if using credit card or paypal as the method of payment, you may be charged additional processing fees.

Board Meetings
